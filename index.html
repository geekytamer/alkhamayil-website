Our Story

Delivering Quality Since 1985

For over three decades, Alkhamayil Dairy has been committed to delivering the finest dairy products to families across the region. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide pure, fresh, and nutritious dairy products while maintaining the highest standards of quality.

Today, we continue to honor that commitment through our innovative production processes and unwavering dedication to excellence. Our state-of-the-art facilities combine traditional dairy-making wisdom with modern technology to ensure every product meets our exceptional standards.
